Accessibility fixes — which to prioritize?

All three fixes are valuable, but implementation effort varies. What's the priority order?

A

Contrast first, then buttons, then motion

Pros: Contrast is easiest (CSS variable change), affects most users immediately.

Cons: Buttons and motion are important for accessibility too.

B

Do all three together

Pros: Complete accessibility pass, no partial implementation.

Cons: Slightly more testing overhead.

C

Buttons first, then contrast, then motion

Cons: Contrast is a quick win that shouldn't wait.
