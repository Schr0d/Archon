Intro hint text on the canvas

What helpful guidance should appear on the canvas when a graph loads? Users need to know what they can do.

A

"Click a node to highlight dependencies. Drag to pan. Scroll to zoom."

Pros: Explains core interactions immediately.

Cons: Three things at once — maybe overwhelming?

B

"Hover over nodes to see connections. Click to inspect details."

Pros: Focuses on discovery, easier to digest.

Cons: Doesn't explain navigation (pan/zoom).

C

"Dependency graph ready. Hover, click, or drag to explore."

Pros: Short, inviting, leaves room for discovery.

Cons: Less explicit about what each action does.
